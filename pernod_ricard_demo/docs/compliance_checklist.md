# Pernod Ricard Japan - Supplier Compliance Checklist

## Pre-Qualification Checklist

### Mandatory Items (all must be YES)
- [ ] Business registration document (登記簿謄本) provided
- [ ] At least one food safety certification (ISO 22000 / FSSC 22000 / HACCP)
- [ ] ISO 9001 certification current and valid
- [ ] Product liability insurance in place
- [ ] No active recalls or regulatory violations in past 3 years

### Japan-Specific Requirements
- [ ] Registered with local health center (保健所) if food-contact materials
- [ ] Compliance with Food Safety Act (食品衛生法)
- [ ] Compliance with Container & Packaging Recycling Act if packaging supplier
- [ ] Tax registration number (法人番号) verified
- [ ] Anti-social forces exclusion declaration (反社会的勢力排除条項) signed

### Environmental & Social
- [ ] Environmental policy documented
- [ ] Waste management procedures in place
- [ ] Worker safety records available
- [ ] No child labor or forced labor

## Annual Review Items
1. Updated certification copies
2. Financial stability assessment
3. Quality performance review (target: >95% pass rate)
4. Delivery performance review (target: >90% on-time)
5. Price benchmarking against market
6. Sustainability progress report

## Red Flags (Automatic Escalation)
- Missing food safety certification for food-contact suppliers
- Reliability score below 0.60
- No audit conducted in past 24 months
- Price anomaly exceeding 3x category maximum
- Lead time exceeding 2x category standard
- Multiple quality failures in consecutive quarters
- Unresolved corrective action requests (CARs)

## Certification Validity
| Certification | Validity Period | Renewal Lead Time |
|--------------|----------------|-------------------|
| ISO 9001 | 3 years | 6 months before expiry |
| ISO 22000 | 3 years | 6 months before expiry |
| FSSC 22000 | 3 years | 6 months before expiry |
| HACCP | Annual review | 3 months before expiry |
| ISO 14001 | 3 years | 6 months before expiry |
| JAS | Annual | 3 months before expiry |
